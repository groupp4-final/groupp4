Basic Information

There is 14 nationally protected areas in Texas that make up around 1.2 million acres of land. These federally protected lands parks, historical monuments, wildlife preserves, national memorials, and recreation areas. While having so much land preserved, Texas ranks 38th of 50 states in terms of national park visits. The Purpose of this website is to inform people on the closest parks to them and provide resources to learn more.
